Scholarships for AFS and InSight students

 

At First Sight exists entirely through the generosity of those who believe in the program.  Tuition income alone cannot support the program, and each year, many of the students who wish to come are not able to afford to pay full tuition.  AFS awards scholarships to deserving students who come from difficult financial backgrounds, but the amount of scholarship money available is very small, compared with the need.

 

Your donation would make it possible for us to award need-based scholarships for young string players.  One hundred percent of donations made to the scholarship fund go to assist deserving students.  Please consider sponsoring a student to At First Sight.

 

  • At First Sight full scholarship: $280
  •  
  • At First Sight half Scholarship: $140
  •  
  • InSight (junior division) full scholarship: $200
  •  
  • InSight (junior division) half scholarship: $100

 

 

Any amount would be gratefully received, and would make a major difference for a young player. (We regret that we are not yet a registered non-profit, and as such, donations to AFS are not tax-deductible.)
